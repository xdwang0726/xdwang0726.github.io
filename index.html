About

I am a first year PhD student in Department of Computer Science at the University of Western Ontario and Vector Insititute supervised by Dr. Robert E. Mercer and Dr. Frank Rudzicz. Previously, I obtained my MSc. at the University of Western Ontario with Dr. Robert E. Mercer, and did my undergraudate at the University of British Columbia. My research interests mostly lie in natural language processing in clinical domain, especially utilizing knowledge graph/graph theory to solve practical problems in healthcare. Full CV
